Certainly, here are the descriptions of common fruits in English:

1. Apple: Apples are rich in fiber and vitamin C, which help maintain digestive health and support the immune system.

2. Banana: Bananas provide energy and are high in potassium, which is beneficial for heart and muscle health, and can also help lower blood pressure.

3. Tomato: Tomatoes contain abundant vitamin C and lycopene, promoting cardiovascular health and reducing blood cholesterol.

4. Orange: Oranges are a good source of vitamin C, contributing to an improved immune system and healthy skin.

5. Grape: Grapes are packed with antioxidants, benefiting heart health and anti-aging.

6. Strawberry: Strawberries are rich in antioxidants and vitamin C, promoting skin health and immune function.

7. Peach: Peaches are rich in vitamin A and fiber, aiding in maintaining vision and intestinal health.

8. Pear: Pears contain vitamin C and dietary fiber, supporting digestion and the immune system.

9. Blueberry: Blueberries are rich in antioxidants, beneficial for brain health and memory.

10. Mango: Mangoes provide vitamin A and C, contributing to vision and skin health.
